About Temple Run 2

Temple Run 2: An Exhilarating Endless Runner Adventure

Temple Run 2 is an action-packed infinite runner where players must flee from relentless enemies while dodging treacherous obstacles. Navigate through perilous cliffs, dense forests, and dark mines as a daring treasure hunter racing to escape with a priceless golden idol. But stay sharp—ferocious demon monkeys are hot on your trail! Are you ready to embark on this heart-pounding adventure?

How to Play Temple Run 2 Online

  • Move: A/D or Left/Right arrow keys to shift lanes or turn.
  • Jump: W or Up arrow key to leap over gaps or obstacles.
  • Slide: S or Down arrow key to slide under barriers.

How to Succeed in Temple Run 2

  • Gather coins to unlock power-ups and upgrades.
  • Anticipate edges and jump early to avoid falling.
  • Enhance character stats for better performance.
  • Time jumps carefully to clear double-jump obstacles.
  • Complete stage objectives to progress and earn rewards.

Can I Play Temple Run 2 on PC?

Yes, you can enjoy Temple Run 2 directly in your browser on Poki without any downloads or installations, making it accessible and free to play.

Does Temple Run 2 Have an Ending?

No, Temple Run 2 is designed as an endless runner, meaning the temple has no finish line. The game continues until your character crashes into an obstacle, falls into water, or is caught by the pursuing demon monkeys.

Is Temple Run 2 Online or Offline?

Playing Temple Run 2 on Poki requires an active internet connection.

What Are the Monsters in Temple Run 2?

The primary adversaries in Temple Run 2 are the Evil Demon Monkeys, sinister creatures that relentlessly chase the player to reclaim the stolen golden idol.

What Is the Power Meter in Temple Run 2?

The power meter fills as you collect coins during a run. Once full, it triggers a temporary boost, granting increased speed and invincibility to help you evade dangers.

How to Upgrade Powers in Temple Run 2?

Visit the "Abilities" menu to enhance specific powers like Shield Duration, Coin Magnet, or Boost Distance by selecting and upgrading your preferred ability.
